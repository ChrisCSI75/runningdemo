Run & Distance From When Additional Info
Monday 5k with Up & Running Up & Running Meet at shop from 5.45, set off at 6.15 A 5K around the town and streets of Darlington. All speeds and abilities catered for with loop backs and rest points (usually each mile). Around 45 minutes or so from setting off to last person back.
Weekly 5K Dolphin Centre Meet at Dolphin Centre (lobby on the left) from 5.45, set off at 6.10. Alternates Wednesday and Thursday. A 5K around the town and streets of Darlington. All speeds and abilities catered for with loop backs and/or catch ups. Pace is dictated by the back runners. Around 30-45 minutes from setting off to finish.
Weekly 10K Dolphin Centre Meet at Dolphin Centre (lobby on the left) from 5.45, set off at 6.10. Alternates Wednesday and Thursday. A 10K around the town and wider area of Darlington. All speeds and abilities catered for with loop backs and/or catch ups. Pace is dictated by the back runners. Around 60-80 minutes from setting off to finish.
Friday Five Miler or Trail Run Dolphin Centre Meet at Dolphin Centre (lobby on the left) from 5.15 to set off at 5.30.
