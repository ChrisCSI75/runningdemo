DARLO RUNNERS

Darlo Runners - a free, friendly and welcoming running group. We're not a club, but a group of all speeds and abilities and no-one is left behind. We meet several times a week and run different lengths for all levels of running ability.

Why Run With Us?

Friendly

A friendly group created by a runner, from a love of running and for runners. Join us for a 5K, 10K, 5 mile or half marathon, or come along on one of our charity runs.

Join us!

Free

No costs, no fees, just running, bants, and socialising. Wear whatever you want, or pick up a Darlo Runners top to spread the word!

Join in!

Fun

We're not just about running, we're about "fun-ing" too, with social events like bowling and meals out - runners gotta have our treats!

Join the fun!
